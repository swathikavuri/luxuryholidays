About Us

LuxuryHolidayDirect.com is a new trading brand within SUNSET TRAVEL LTD. The Company has been trading for over a quarter of a century where from our modest beginnings we have grown into a successful financially secure company employing over 60 members of staff (The Luxury Holidays Direct Team). Our head office is situated in our own premises in Croydon, Surrey (Sunset House). We are ABTA licensed and ATOL bonded, whereby if you''ve booked and contracted either a complete air holiday package or just a flight only, ATOL protects you from losing money or being stranded abroad if the tour operator goes out of business. This bond requires a proven track record of long-term trading. (For further information please visit www.caa.co.uk/atol where our ATOL Number is 2886). As the name suggests this new brand has been created for the sole purpose of offering consumers a Luxury Holiday Experience at affordable prices.

LuxuryHolidaysDirect.com is your one stop shop for all types of luxury holidays to include City Breaks, Beach Stays, Cultural Tours, Weddings, Honeymoons and whether it be a Seasonal Family Holiday to celebrating that Special Occasion abroad. This site features over 400 luxury hotels & resorts in faraway destinations and we have in 2010 expanded our offering introducing Morocco and Zanzibar.

Our Travel Consultants are recruited and trained to our very high standards in order to provide the specialist Destination & Resort knowledge, so when formulating your luxury holiday we will ensure that an experienced Travel Consultant is on hand to guide you through the planning and booking procedure. Further to ensure all the featured hotels & resorts are adhering to our high standards, our product department use key indicators such in 'Health & Safety', 'Service Expectations', 'Quality of Hotel & Rooms' and 'Overall Experience' to determine whether that property remains within our portfolio.
